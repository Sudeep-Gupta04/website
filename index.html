About Our IIITV

Indian Institute Of Information Technology (IIITV)

Indian Institute of Information Technology Vadodara (IIIT Vadodara) is an Institute of National Importance established in 2013 under a Public-Private Partnership (PPP) model. It focuses on providing quality education and research in Information Technology and allied areas. Located in Vadodara, Gujarat, it is known for its innovative curriculum and industry-driven approach.

Indian Institute of Information Technology Vadodara (IIIT Vadodara) offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in areas like B.Tech, M.Tech, and Ph.D., under the aegis of the Ministry of Education, Government of India. The institute, established in 2013, is located in Vadodara, Gujarat, and is recognized for its excellence in education and research in Information Technology. It provides an industry-driven curriculum to equip students for the global tech landscape.
