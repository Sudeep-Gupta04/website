Bachelor of Technology ( BTech )

Programme Structure : ( BTech )
The Bachelor of Technology (BTech) is a professional undergraduate engineering program designed to equip students with the technical skills and knowledge needed to thrive in the ever-evolving world of technology and innovation. Spanning four years and eight semesters, the program focuses on a wide range of engineering disciplines, providing a strong foundation in scientific principles and practical applications.



Eligibility Criteria :

  1. Completion of 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as core subjects with minimum 75% marks in any respective board.
  2. A qualifying score in national or state-level entrance exams like JEE Main, or equivalent.

Course Information

BTech is Full-time Technical UG course. The duration is 4 years ( 8 semester ).

BTech subjects include Database Management systems, software engineering, Computer Networking, Operating systems, Web technology and computer languages such as HTML & Css, C, C++, C#, Java.

FAQ

What are academic degree programs offered by IIIT Vadodara

IIIT Vadodara currently offered B Tech,M Tech and PhD Programmes.

How do i get admission to BTech program at IIIT Vadodara?

Admissions to B Tech program is through the Joint Seat Allocation Authority (JOSSA) constituted by the Government of India. The Institute does not conduct its own entrance examination. The admissions are purely based on the All India Ranking (AIR) announced by the JEE main examination committee (conducted by National Testing agency). Admission is through the centralized counselling process. Reservation as per mandate of GoI applicable to SC, ST, OBC, PH, EWS and others is applicable. The seat matrix provides a breakup of the seats in different categories.

What are the placement opportunies for graduates?

The Institute placement cell headed by the placement officer works towards student placement and training.
