Indian Institute of Information Technology Vadodara (IIIT Vadodara) is one of the premier institutes for higher education in Information Technology in India. Established in the year 2013, it operates under the mentorship of DA-IICT Gandhinagar and functions as an Institute of National Importance, established by an Act of Parliament. IIIT Vadodara aims to provide high-quality education, training, and research in the field of IT and allied disciplines. Currently, IIIT Vadodara offers B.Tech, M.Tech, and Ph.D. programs in disciplines like Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) and Information Technology (IT), focusing on cutting-edge technologies and industry-relevant skills. These programs are designed to foster academic excellence and holistic development, equipping students to thrive in both academic and industry environments. The institute operates from its permanent campus located at Dumad, Vadodara, which is equipped with modern infrastructure, spacious classrooms, well-equipped labs, and recreational facilities. The convenient location ensures accessibility to the city center, making it an ideal place for students and faculty. IIIT Vadodara’s Vision: To become a hub of innovation, research, and academic excellence in the field of Information Technology, contributing to the technological growth of the nation and beyond. The institute’s governance is backed by a dedicated board comprising representatives from the Ministry of Education, Government of India, the Gujarat State Government, and leading industries. IIIT Vadodara is known for its strong industry-academia collaboration, which enhances the learning experience by aligning the curriculum with real-world demands. With an aim for all-round development, the faculty members of IIIT Vadodara are highly qualified and committed to nurturing students through innovative teaching methods, hands-on projects, and research initiatives. The non-teaching staff also works tirelessly to provide a supportive environment for the students. The institute consistently endeavors to cultivate a culture of discipline, innovation, and ethical responsibility, fostering leaders and innovators for tomorrow. IIIT Vadodara’s ecosystem provides opportunities for students to engage in meaningful academic pursuits while participating in extracurricular and co-curricular activities. Its growing reputation reflects the institute’s commitment to nurturing the next generation of IT professionals and researchers.

Our Motto Vision To be a dynamic and innovative hub for education, research, and industry collaboration in technology and allied fields, shaping socially responsible technocrats, leaders, and entrepreneurs. IIIT Vadodara aspires to address contemporary global challenges through cutting-edge education and research, nurturing talent for the evolving needs of industry, government, and society. The institute aims to partner with industry and esteemed institutions to provide students with the expertise and experience needed to excel in their chosen domains. Mission To deliver quality education and conduct pioneering research while fostering a learner-centric environment equipped with state-of-the-art infrastructure. By embracing innovative teaching methods and global partnerships, IIIT Vadodara strives to produce socially responsible professionals, innovators, and leaders in technology and information sciences, establishing itself as a premier institution. Belief We believe in the power of diversity and interdisciplinary collaboration to enrich learning experiences. IIIT Vadodara fosters an inclusive and innovative culture, embracing modern advancements to stay relevant to stakeholders. Our core values emphasize openness, fairness, and active participation to create an environment conducive to growth and learning. Our Values and Principles IIIT Vadodara is built on four pillars: Academic Excellence, Holistic Development, Global Relevance, and Contemporary Innovation.
